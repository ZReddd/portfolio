City of Adelaide’s Age Structure

This project presents a data visualization created with Power BI, using demographic data sourced from the City of Adelaide’s official website. The focus is on the population structure of Adelaide as of 2021, segmented by age. In the bar chart, individuals aged over 50 are represented in orange, while those 50 and below are depicted in blue.

This visualization aims to provide healthcare and aged care services in Adelaide with a clearer understanding of age distribution within the community, enabling these sectors to strategically allocate resources and attention to relevant age groups.
